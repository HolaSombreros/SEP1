Description

ColorIT, a small Danish company, conceives and designs IT systems for particular customers.

History

All began in 2010, when two people met and made history. Jack Turquoise and Ronald Green, who worked together for several years, came to the conclusion that they are going to build a company from the ground. Passionated about computers and software, Jack and Ronald figured out that the Danish Software Industry is lacking a business that design and conceives IT systems. Therefore, they turned their dreams into reality. Now, ColorIT is one of the most recognised Software Companies in Denmark as they expanded their technology offerings and service solutions.
