Colour IT

Description

ColourIT, a small Danish company, conceives and designs IT systems for particular customers. Its focus is more on practical skills than computer science fundamentals. History

All began in 2010, when two people met and made history. Jack Turquoise and Ronald Green, who worked together for several years, came to the conclusion that they are going to build a company from the ground. Passionated about computers and software, Jack and Ronald figured out that the Danish Software Industry is lacking a business that design and conceives IT systems. Therefore, they turned their dreams into reality. Now, ColourIT is one of the most recognised Software Companies in Denmark as they expanded their technology offerings and service solutions.

Projects & Team

It is said that a close and strong team is the foundation of a high-performance business. Also, a good cooperation and a good team ethic are accountable for a successful business. ColourIT team follows a set of principles: good communication, focus on goals, equal contribution and diversification. Therefore, the team is coordinating both Waterfall and Scrum projects for certain customers. These projects comprise entertainment, artificial intelligence games and simulation, personal finance projects.
